# Proof Boundary

## Purpose
This document states exactly what AGIF v1 is supposed to prove and what it is not supposed to prove.

## Positive Claim AGIF v1 Is Allowed to Make
AGIF v1 may claim success only if it shows that a governed multi-cell software fabric can run locally and use:
- cells
- tissues
- skill descriptors
- shared workspace
- local and fabric memory
- bounded adaptation
- utility and motivation signals
- governance
- replay
- rollback
- trust
- quarantine
- lifecycle-aware growth paths

inside a bounded document/workflow proof domain.

AGIF v1 may describe itself as:
- a software-first architectural proof
- local-first
- resource-aware
- anti-bloat by design

AGIF v1 may not describe itself as an achieved AGI system.

## The Required Proof Domain
- The proof domain is document/workflow intelligence only.
- The first real tissue system is the finance document workflow.
- The proof domain must stay structured, stateful, and auditable.
- The workflow must involve more than one role and more than one workflow step.

## What AGIF v1 Must Demonstrate

### 1. Local Runnable Prototype
- AGIF v1 must run locally in this standalone project workspace.
- It must not depend on the old repo at runtime.

### 2. Real Multi-Cell Structure
- The system must show real separation between cells, tissues, workspace, memory, and governance roles.
- The proof must be more than a single flat script with renamed components.

### 3. Bounded Adaptation
- The system must show bounded learning, reuse, or adaptation behavior in the proof domain.
- That behavior must be constrained by explicit limits and review controls.

### 4. Memory Discipline
- The system must show that useful memory is retained selectively.
- Raw logs may exist as evidence, but raw logs alone do not count as long-term memory.

### 5. Governance and Recovery
- The system must show replay, rollback, trust, and quarantine behavior on bounded cases.
- The system must be able to reject or contain bad outputs or bad promoted artifacts.

### 6. Benchmark Evidence
- The system must produce benchmark evidence that compares AGIF behavior against simpler baselines.
- The benchmark must be reproducible locally.

### 7. Resource-Aware Operation
- The system must fit the target machine profile.
- It must stay within the locked runtime and storage limits for the defined proof runs.

## What AGIF v1 Is Not Allowed to Claim
AGIF v1 is not allowed to claim that it proves:
- AGI has been achieved
- large-scale emergence has been achieved
- massive autonomous self-organization has been achieved
- real-world embodied intelligence has been achieved
- safety at real-world hardware scale has been achieved
- open-ended general reasoning across arbitrary domains has been achieved

## What Counts as In Bounds
The following are in bounds for AGIF v1:
- software-first execution
- bounded document workflows
- multi-step finance document handling
- specialization by cell role
- memory retention and reuse under review
- governed routing and recovery
- benchmarked comparison against flatter alternatives
- evidence packaging for reproduction

## What Counts as Out of Bounds
The following are out of bounds for AGIF v1:
- public release packaging
- production deployment promises
- claims about millions of real hardware cells
- claims about embodied autonomy in uncontrolled environments
- claims about unrestricted self-improvement
- broad AGI claims beyond the document/workflow proof boundary

## Required Evidence Package
AGIF v1 is only within proof if it can eventually produce:
- a runnable local prototype
- a research paper
- benchmark evidence
- a reproducibility package

If one of those deliverables is missing, the finish line is not met.

## Boundary Discipline Rule
When later phases produce results, all summaries must clearly separate:
- locally verified facts
- assumed future path
- architectural intent

This prevents AGIF v1 from overstating what was actually demonstrated.
